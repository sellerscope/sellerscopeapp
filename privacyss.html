Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Seller Scope ("we," "us," or "our") is a sales analytics application that connects to Amazon Selling Partner APIs to help Amazon sellers monitor their sales performance. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Seller Scope, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Amazon Account Data: Sales data, order history, units sold, revenue figures, and product information retrieved through the Amazon Selling Partner API (SP-API) on your behalf.
  • Account Information: Your name and email address when you register for access to Seller Scope.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the application, including pages viewed and features accessed.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Display your Amazon sales data within the Seller Scope dashboard
  • Calculate and present sales totals by day, month, and year
  • Enable date-based order lookup and product purchase history
  • Maintain and improve the application
  • Communicate with you about your account

3. Amazon SP-API Data

Seller Scope accesses your Amazon selling data through Amazon's Selling Partner API. This data is used exclusively to power your personal dashboard. We do not sell, share, transfer, or use your Amazon data for any purpose other than displaying it back to you within the application.

Your Amazon credentials and refresh tokens are stored securely and are never shared with third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information or Amazon data with any third parties, except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Seller Scope, our users, or the public
  • With your explicit consent

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS/HTTPS
  • Secure storage of API credentials and tokens
  • Access controls limiting who can access your data
  • Regular security reviews of our systems

6. Data Retention

We retain your account information and cached sales data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at hello@sellerscopeapp.com.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke Amazon API access at any time through your Amazon Seller Central account
  • Opt out of any marketing communications

8. Cookies

Seller Scope may use cookies and similar technologies to maintain your session and preferences. We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking purposes.

9. Children's Privacy

Seller Scope is intended for use by Amazon sellers and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a new effective date. Continued use of Seller Scope after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
